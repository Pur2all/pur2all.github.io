Francesco Migliaro

francesco.migliaro@phd.unict.it

Hi, I’m Francesco! I’m a Ph.D. student of the Crypto group at University of Catania. I am supervised by Dario Catalano.

My research interests are (for now) mostly related to theoretical aspects of cryptography and public key cryptography with advanced functionalities. You can find a list of my works on my publications page.

I obtained both my master’s and bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from University of Salerno. My studies were focused mostly on Cryptography, Information Theory and theoretical foundations of Computer Science, like computability theory and complexity theory.

See my CV for more details.
